Modular Centre of Mass Device

Centre of Mass Measurement System

A precision modular system designed to accurately determine the centre of mass for satellite assemblies and mechanical systems. This device is critical for balance, structural integrity verification, and payload integration testing in space applications.

Project Scope

  • Designed modular measurement platform compatible with multiple assembly configurations
  • Manufactured precision components to tight tolerances (±0.5mm)
  • Integrated load cells and measurement electronics for accurate data acquisition
  • Validated measurement accuracy against theoretical calculations
  • Documented procedures for field deployment and calibration

Technical Specifications

  • Measurement Range: Up to 5 kg assemblies
  • Accuracy: ±2 grams centre of mass uncertainty
  • Resolution: 0.1 mm positional accuracy
  • Design: Modular three-axis suspension system with load cells
  • Materials: Aluminum frame, precision ball bearings, stainless steel fixtures

Manufacturing & Assembly

  • CNC milling for main frame and precision fixtures
  • Vertical mill operations for custom brackets and adapters
  • Metal lathe work for bearing housings and cylindrical components
  • Hand assembly and calibration of load cell arrays
  • Quality assurance through coordinate measurement machine (CMM) verification

Key Features

  • Modular design allows quick reconfiguration for different payload shapes and masses
  • Integrated data logging system captures X, Y, Z force measurements
  • Wireless connectivity for real-time monitoring and analysis
  • Thermal compensation for temperature-dependent measurement drift
  • Portable frame enables field testing and satellite integration verification
