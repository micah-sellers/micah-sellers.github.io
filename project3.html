STEM Outreach & Education

Founding Co-President of the University of Alberta Mechatronics Club, leading a team of 7 executive members overseeing a club of ~100 students. Dedicated to aerospace engineering education, hands-on project experience, and inspiring the next generation of engineers.

Leadership Responsibilities

  • Founded and established governance structure for the Mechatronics Club (from zero to 100+ members)
  • Lead executive team of 7 members in strategic planning and operations
  • Organize workshops, seminars, and talks by industry professionals
  • Coordinate hands-on projects including satellite design, drone development, and robotics challenges
  • Manage club budget, events, and external partnerships
  • Mentor junior members and facilitate knowledge transfer

Educational Initiatives

  • Aerospace Education Sessions: Interactive demos and explanations covering satellite mechanics, CubeSat design, and space systems
  • Hands-On Workshops: CAD design (SolidWorks/Onshape), PCB design, fabrication techniques, and control systems
  • Project-Based Learning: Students participate in Ex-Alta 3 development, gaining real-world engineering experience
  • Outreach to High Schools: STEM promotion and encouraging students to pursue engineering disciplines
  • Equipment Access: Provide instruction on CNC, mills, lathes, and other fabrication tools

Impact & Outcomes

  • Grew club membership from 0 to 100+ active members in one year
  • Successfully executed satellite design educational program
  • Fostered collaborative environment promoting hands-on learning
  • Connected students with industry professionals and mentors
  • Contributed to increased interest in mechatronics and aerospace at University of Alberta
  • Developed future leaders and engineers through mentorship

Key Skills Demonstrated

  • Team Leadership & Project Management
  • Communication & Public Speaking
  • Strategic Planning & Operations
  • Mentorship & Knowledge Transfer
  • Technical Expertise (Aerospace & Mechatronics)
  • Budget Management & Resource Allocation
