Ex-Alta 3 Satellite

AlbertaSat Ex-Alta 3 CubeSat

Mechanical systems design, simulation, prototyping, manufacturing, and testing for the Ex-Alta 3 CubeSat. This is Alberta's response to the Canadian Satellite Design Challenge and represents cutting-edge small satellite technology.

Role & Contributions

  • Simulated mechanical systems using ANSYS for structural validation
  • Prototyped and manufactured mechanical components using CNC and metal lathe
  • Designed modular centre of mass determination device for full assembly testing
  • Tested and validated mechanical systems for deployment reliability
  • Collaborated with electrical and software teams on integrated design

Technical Details

  • Tools: SolidWorks, Onshape, ANSYS, CNC/Lathe
  • Scope: CubeSat mechanical platform for satellite operations
  • Team: Multi-disciplinary (Mechanical, Electrical, Software)
  • Timeline: Sep 2024 – Present (ongoing)

Key Achievements

  • Created precision measurement system for satellite assembly validation
  • Manufactured high-precision components for space-qualified systems
  • Validated design through simulation and testing protocols
  • Supported STEM outreach by demonstrating aerospace engineering applications
